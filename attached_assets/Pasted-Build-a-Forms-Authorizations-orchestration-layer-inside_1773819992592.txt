Build a “Forms & Authorizations” orchestration layer inside the Staff Portal Client Session view so staff can manage all required Jotform forms, PandaDoc documents, and document workflow steps from one place.

Create a dedicated section called “Forms & Authorizations” inside each client session.

Group items into three categories:
	1.	Intake & Information (Jotform)

	•	Vital Statistics Information
	•	Social Security Number Request for Death Certificate Filing
	•	Funeral Service Details Agreement
	•	Preplanning / Intake

	2.	Legal Authorizations (PandaDoc)

	•	Authorization for Removal and Transfer
	•	Cremation Authorization
	•	Authorization to Embalm
	•	Terms & Conditions

Important workflow note:
Authorization for Removal and Transfer is its own PandaDoc document, but it is sent as part of the same PandaDoc authorization workflow as the related authorization documents. Do not treat it as a separate completely unrelated workflow; treat it as a required legal document inside the broader authorization bundle/process.
	3.	Documents & Filing

	•	Jotform-generated PDFs
	•	Signed authorization PDFs
	•	Death certificate workflow status
	•	SSN handling status
